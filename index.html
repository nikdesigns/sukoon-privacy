Privacy Policy — Sukoon Breaks

A peaceful focus timer for macOS
We don’t collect any personal data.
Last updated: 28 November 2025
App: Sukoon Breaks (macOS)

1. Overview

Sukoon Breaks is designed with privacy in mind. The app runs entirely on your Mac and does not collect, transmit or sell any personal data.

All reminders, timer settings and tip purchases stay between you, your Mac, and Apple’s systems.

2. Data we do not collect

Sukoon Breaks does not collect or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contacts, calendars or files from your Mac

Your break interval, reminder mode, and other settings are stored locally on your device using standard macOS preferences.

3. Notifications

Sukoon Breaks uses macOS notifications only to remind you when it’s time to take a break.

  • No notification content is sent to our servers (we don’t have any).
  • You can turn notifications on or off at any time from macOS System Settings.

4. Tips & payments

Sukoon Breaks offers optional in-app tips to support the app. All payments are processed securely by Apple via the App Store.

  • We do not have access to your full payment details.
  • Apple may share anonymous sales reports (for example, number of tips per country), but these reports do not identify you personally.

5. Third-party services

Sukoon Breaks does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks or external tracking services.

The only third party involved is Apple, for:

  • App distribution via the Mac App Store
  • Payment processing for in-app tips
  • Notification delivery handled by macOS

6. Your choices

  • You can pause or stop reminders at any time from within the app.
  • You can disable notifications for Sukoon Breaks in macOS System Settings.
  • If you uninstall the app, your local settings and data will be removed from your device.

7. Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in a significant way, we will update this page and bump the “Last updated” date above. The app will always remain privacy-friendly and minimal.
